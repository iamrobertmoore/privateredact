Redact sensitive documents.
And prove they stayed private.

Cover up names, numbers and anything else sensitive in a document. Your file is handled entirely on your device, and the text is read by AI sealed inside tamper-proof hardware — sent straight there from your browser, not through our servers — so the details never leave your hands.

Why it's safe to use this
  • Your document is opened and blacked out right here on your device. The file itself is never uploaded to us.
  • To find what's sensitive, the text (never the file) is sent to Nillion's private AI, which runs inside sealed, tamper-proof hardware that not even Nillion or the cloud provider hosting it can see into. It goes straight from your browser to that hardware — it doesn't pass through our servers (we only ever hand out a short-lived access key), and the panel under your redaction proves how each run was handled.
  • Every scan is cryptographically verified against the enclave's own hardware attestation, and the result is shown right below your redaction.
